Formula Reference

Every formula used anywhere in these solutions, grouped by topic. Each one is tagged so you know whether you already have it in the exam.

on official sheet  =  already printed on the professor's Derivatives & Integrals sheet or the Laplace Transform tables — you don't need to copy it.

bring yourself  =  not on any official sheet — put it on your own handwritten A4 page. The Cheat Sheet collects all of these (and the trivial ones too) in a dense, printable layout.
