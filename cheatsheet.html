Derivatives ŌĆö rules

  • O $(cf)'=cf',\ (f\pm g)'=f'\pm g'$
  • O $(fg)'=f'g+fg'$
  • O $\left(\frac fg\right)'=\frac{f'g-fg'}{g^2}$
  • O $\frac{d}{dx}f(g(x))=f'(g)\,g'$
  • O $\frac{d}{dx}x^n=nx^{n-1}$, $\ \frac{d}{dx}c=0$
  • O $\frac{d}{dx}\sqrt u=\frac{u'}{2\sqrt u}$
  • O $\frac{d}{dx}\frac1x=-\frac1{x^2}$

Derivatives ŌĆö exp / log / trig

  • O $\frac{d}{dx}e^{x}=e^x,\ \frac{d}{dx}e^{g}=g'e^{g}$
  • O $\frac{d}{dx}a^x=a^x\ln a$
  • O $\frac{d}{dx}\ln x=\frac1x,\ \frac{d}{dx}\ln g=\frac{g'}{g}$
  • O $\frac{d}{dx}\log_a x=\frac{1}{x\ln a}$
  • O $\sin'=\cos,\ \cos'=-\sin$
  • O $\tan' t=\frac{1}{\cos^2 t},\ \cot' t=-\frac{1}{\sin^2 t}$
  • O $\arctan'u=\frac{u'}{1+u^2},\ \operatorname{arccot}'u=-\frac{u'}{1+u^2}$
  • O $\arcsin'u=\frac{u'}{\sqrt{1-u^2}},\ \arccos'u=-\frac{u'}{\sqrt{1-u^2}}$

Integrals ŌĆö basic

  • O $\int x^n dx=\frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1}+c\ (n\neq-1)$
  • O $\int\frac1x dx=\ln|x|+c$
  • O $\int\frac{1}{ax+b}dx=\frac1a\ln|ax+b|+c$
  • O $\int e^u du=e^u+c$
  • O $\int a^u du=\frac{a^u}{\ln a}+c$
  • O $\int\ln u\,du=u\ln u-u+c$
  • O $\int u\,e^u du=(u-1)e^u+c$
  • O $\int\frac{1}{u\ln u}du=\ln|\ln u|+c$

Integrals ŌĆö trig

  • O $\int\cos u\,du=\sin u+c$
  • O $\int\frac{1}{\cos^2 u}\,du=\tan u+c$
  • O $\int\frac{1}{\sin^2 u}\,du=-\cot u+c$
  • O $\int\tan u\,du=-\ln|\cos u|+c$
  • O $\int\cot u\,du=\ln|\sin u|+c$
  • Ōśģ $\int\frac{1}{\cos u}\,du=\frac12\ln\left|\frac{1+\sin u}{1-\sin u}\right|+c$
  • Ōśģ $\int\frac{1}{\sin u}\,du=\frac12\ln\left|\frac{1-\cos u}{1+\cos u}\right|+c$

Linear ODE ŌĆö recipe (WS6ŌĆō7)

  • Ōśģ $ay''+by'+cy=g\Rightarrow y=y_h+y_p$
  • Ōśģ char. eqn $ar^2+br+c=0$, $r=\frac{-b\pm\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}$
  • Ōśģ real $r_1\ne r_2$: $y_h=c_1e^{r_1t}+c_2e^{r_2t}$
  • Ōśģ double $r$: $y_h=(c_1+c_2t)e^{rt}$
  • Ōśģ $\alpha\pm\beta i$: $y_h=e^{\alpha t}(c_1\cos\beta t+c_2\sin\beta t)$

  • Ōśģ $g=P_n(t)\to$ full degree-$n$ polynomial
  • Ōśģ $g=\cos bt$ or $\sin bt\to A\cos bt+B\sin bt$
  • Ōśģ $g=e^{at}P_n\to e^{at}\cdot$(deg-$n$ poly)
  • Ōśģ $g=e^{at}\cos bt\to e^{at}(A\cos bt+B\sin bt)$
  • Ōśģ sums of pieces $\to$ sum of guesses
  • Ōśģ Resonance: ├Ś$t^s$, $s=$ multiplicity of matching root ($0,1,2$)

Variation of parameters

Partials, gradient, tangent (WS8)

  • Ōśģ $\frac{\partial f}{\partial x_i}$: differentiate in $x_i$, others fixed
  • Ōśģ $\nabla f=(f_{x_1},\dots,f_{x_n})$
  • Ōśģ dir. deriv. $\frac{\partial f}{\partial v}=\nabla f\cdot v$ (v as given)
  • Ōśģ tangent plane $z=f(x_0)+\nabla f(x_0)\cdot(x-x_0)$
  • Ōśģ diff'able $\Leftrightarrow \lim\frac{|f(x)-f(x_0)-\nabla f(x_0)\cdot(x-x_0)|}{\lVert x-x_0\rVert}=0$
  • Ōśģ diff'able $\Rightarrow$ continuous & partials exist; converse false
  • Ōśģ disprove diff'ability: show not continuous (path test)
